Reed College Consent to Participate in Research

Sentence Reading Experiment

You are invited to participate in a study designed to measure reading behaviors and comprehension, conducted by Christy Lei under the supervision of Professor Enriqueta Canseco-Gonzalez at Reed College.

The results of this study will be used to better understand how the brain processes different types of sentences. This experiment will take approximately 30 minutes of your time, and you will receive $10 via online payment in return for your participation.

If you decide to participate in this study, you will be asked to read sentences on a computer screen and complete a survey at the end of the experiment. There is no risk or benefit associated with your participation in this study.

The data collected in this experiment will be kept confidential, and the data will never be presented or published along with any identifying information that could be linked back to you.

Your participation in this study is entirely voluntary. You may refuse to answer individual questions or to engage in individual activities. You may also discontinue participation in this study at any time by exiting your browser. We are happy to answer any questions about the study or the information contained in this form.
